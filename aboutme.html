About Me

Kai Schwennicke

Theoretical Chemistry

Postdoctoral Researcher and Lecturer at the University of Sydney

Bio:

Dr. Kai Schwennicke is a theoretical chemists from Califoria who is currently working as a Postdoctoral Researcher and Lecturer at the University of Sydney. In 2018, he recieved his B.S. in from the University of Califoria in Santa Barbara. In 2024, he earned is Ph.D. in Chemistry from the Universiy of California in San Diego under the supervision and guidence of Prof. Joel Yuen-Zhou. His dissertation explored light-dressed matter systems with applications in Floquet engineering, topological chiraloptics, and molecular polaritonics. At the start of July 2024, he joined the group of Prof. Ivan Kassal . His current rearch is focused on the developement of analog quantum simulators. He also teaches statistcal mechanics at the University of Sydney.

Hobbies:

Growing in a sleepy beachtown on the coast of California and being raised by two avid surfers, Kai was instilled with a love for the outdoors at a young age. When not in lab or teaching, you can find him surfing at the beach or hiking a local trail. Kai currently lives in Sydney, NSW.
